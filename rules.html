These forums are purely for fun and entertainment, therefore, it is very important that users follow these rules:

#1 - Be civil. Treat people how you want to be treated and do not get personal.

#2 - No spoilers. Do not spoil new episodes, or events further into the season in episode threads. If your discussion contains any spoilers, make sure that you include a spoiler warning in your thread title.

#3 - No hate speech. This is completely unacceptable in the Survivor Central forums and will result in an instant ban. A statement that carries no meaning other than an expression of hatred, which is intended to demean and brutalize another by the use of cruel and derogatory language.

#4 - No political debates. Survivor Central is a TV SHOW discussion platform, not a news site. Therefore, there should be no discussion regarding the political beliefs of users.

#5 - Help us out. If there is a problem or a user that you would like to report, please email a moderator with the situation you have encountered. This will help to make Survivor Central a better place for all users
